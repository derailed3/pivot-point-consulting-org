# Workflow: New Hire Onboarding

## Purpose

The new hire onboarding workflow ensures every new PPC team member has a consistent, welcoming, and thorough introduction to the organization.
Effective onboarding accelerates time-to-productivity, reinforces PPC culture, and ensures compliance with required training (including HIPAA).

## Trigger

- **Trigger Event:** New employee start date confirmed (offer accepted)
- **Trigger Source:** HR notification / [PLACEHOLDER: HRIS system]
- **Frequency:** Per new hire

## Roles

| Role | Responsibility in Workflow | Person/Team |
|------|---------------------------|-------------|
| HR Coordinator | Manages logistics, compliance paperwork, and system access | [PLACEHOLDER] |
| Hiring Manager | Welcomes the new hire, sets role expectations, assigns initial work | [PLACEHOLDER] |
| Assigned Mentor / Buddy | Provides day-to-day guidance, answers questions, supports cultural integration | [PLACEHOLDER] |
| IT Administrator | Provisions equipment, accounts, and tool access | [PLACEHOLDER] |
| New Hire | Completes required training, engages with onboarding materials | N/A |

## Inputs

- Signed offer letter and employment agreement
- New hire personal information (collected securely via [PLACEHOLDER: HRIS system])
- Role description and team assignment
- Equipment and access requirements for the role
- Onboarding schedule template

## Process Steps -- Timeline

### Pre-Start (1-2 Weeks Before Day 1)

#### Step 1: Provision Equipment

- **Owner:** IT Administrator
- **Description:** Order and configure the new hire's laptop, peripherals, and any role-specific hardware.
Ship equipment to arrive before the start date (for remote employees) or stage at the office.
- **Tools/Systems:** [PLACEHOLDER: IT asset management system]
- **SLA:** Equipment ready 2 business days before start date

#### Step 2: Create Accounts and Access

- **Owner:** IT Administrator / HR Coordinator
- **Description:** Create accounts for all required systems:
  - GitHub organization invite (for knowledge base access)
  - [PLACEHOLDER: email system] account
  - [PLACEHOLDER: communication tool] account
  - [PLACEHOLDER: project management tool] account
  - [PLACEHOLDER: time tracking tool] account
  - [PLACEHOLDER: HRIS system] profile
Do not provision access to client systems until HIPAA training is complete and a BAA is in place for the specific engagement.
- **Tools/Systems:** All listed systems above
- **SLA:** Accounts ready by start date

#### Step 3: Send Welcome Email

- **Owner:** Hiring Manager
- **Description:** Send a welcome email to the new hire including:
  - Start date, time, and location (or virtual meeting link)
  - Day 1 agenda
  - Who to contact with questions before Day 1
  - Any pre-reading materials (e.g., company website, recent blog posts)
  - Equipment delivery tracking information (if remote)
- **Tools/Systems:** Email
- **SLA:** 5 business days before start date

### Day 1

#### Step 4: Orientation Session

- **Owner:** HR Coordinator
- **Description:** Conduct the orientation session covering:
  - Welcome and company overview
  - PPC mission, values, and culture
  - Organizational structure and key contacts
  - Benefits overview and enrollment instructions
  - Payroll and time tracking setup
- **Tools/Systems:** [PLACEHOLDER: video conferencing tool], onboarding presentation
- **SLA:** First 2 hours of Day 1

#### Step 5: Handbook and Policies Review

- **Owner:** HR Coordinator
- **Description:** Walk the new hire through critical policies:
  - Employee handbook
  - Acceptable use policy
  - Information security policy
  - Data handling and privacy requirements
  - Code of conduct
Obtain signed acknowledgment of policy review.
- **Tools/Systems:** Knowledge base (`policies/` directory), [PLACEHOLDER: e-signature tool]
- **SLA:** Day 1

#### Step 6: Tool Access Verification

- **Owner:** IT Administrator / New Hire
- **Description:** Verify the new hire can access all provisioned systems.
Walk through login procedures, MFA setup, and password management.
Troubleshoot any access issues immediately.
- **Tools/Systems:** All provisioned systems
- **SLA:** Day 1 afternoon

### Week 1

#### Step 7: Team Introductions

- **Owner:** Hiring Manager
- **Description:** Introduce the new hire to:
  - Immediate team members (1:1 or small group)
  - Key cross-functional contacts (account leads, practice leads)
  - Executive leadership (brief introduction)
Schedule informal coffee chats or virtual meet-and-greets.
- **Tools/Systems:** [PLACEHOLDER: communication tool], calendar
- **SLA:** By end of Week 1

#### Step 8: HIPAA Training

- **Owner:** HR Coordinator / Compliance
- **Description:** Enroll the new hire in HIPAA training.
Training must cover:
  - HIPAA Privacy Rule fundamentals
  - HIPAA Security Rule and safeguards
  - PHI identification and handling
  - Breach reporting procedures
  - PPC-specific policies for ePHI
The new hire must pass the training assessment before accessing any client systems containing ePHI.
- **Tools/Systems:** [PLACEHOLDER: LMS or training platform]
- **SLA:** Completed by end of Week 1

#### Step 9: Knowledge Base Onboarding

- **Owner:** Hiring Manager / Assigned Mentor
- **Description:** Walk the new hire through the PPC knowledge base:
  - Review `docs/tutorials/getting-started.md`
  - Explain the directory structure and how to find content
  - Demonstrate how to contribute (branching, PRs, templates)
  - Introduce Claude Code skills (if applicable to their role)
  - Show the BTV quality process
- **Tools/Systems:** Knowledge base, Git, Claude Code
- **SLA:** By end of Week 1

#### Step 10: Mentor Assignment

- **Owner:** Hiring Manager
- **Description:** Formally assign a mentor or buddy from the team.
The mentor should:
  - Be available for daily questions during the first month
  - Schedule weekly 1:1 check-ins for the first 90 days
  - Help the new hire navigate PPC culture and unwritten norms
  - Provide feedback on early work product
- **Tools/Systems:** Calendar, [PLACEHOLDER: communication tool]
- **SLA:** Assigned by Day 2, first 1:1 by end of Week 1

### Month 1

#### Step 11: Role-Specific Training

- **Owner:** Hiring Manager
- **Description:** Provide training tailored to the new hire's role:
  - Consultants: EHR platform training (Epic, Oracle Health, etc.), client engagement protocols
  - Project managers: PPC project lifecycle workflows, status reporting, risk management
  - Business development: Proposal process, CRM usage, pricing models
  - Operations: Knowledge base maintenance, quality processes, compliance management
- **Tools/Systems:** Role-specific tools and training materials
- **SLA:** Ongoing through Month 1

#### Step 12: First Project or Task Assignment

- **Owner:** Hiring Manager
- **Description:** Assign a meaningful first project or task that:
  - Is achievable within the first month
  - Provides exposure to real PPC work
  - Has a defined scope and clear success criteria
  - Includes collaboration with existing team members
- **Tools/Systems:** [PLACEHOLDER: project management tool]
- **SLA:** Assignment within first 2 weeks; completion by end of Month 1

#### Step 13: 30-Day Check-In

- **Owner:** Hiring Manager
- **Description:** Conduct a structured 30-day check-in covering:
  - How the new hire is feeling about the role and the organization
  - What is going well and what could be improved
  - Feedback on the onboarding experience
  - Clarification on role expectations and goals
  - Discussion of any training gaps
Document the check-in and any action items.
- **Tools/Systems:** [PLACEHOLDER: HRIS system] or meeting notes
- **SLA:** Between Day 25 and Day 35

## Outputs

- Fully provisioned and oriented new team member
- Signed policy acknowledgments on file
- HIPAA training certificate on file
- 30/60/90 day check-in documentation
- Mentor relationship established

## Definition of Done

- [ ] New hire has access to all required systems
- [ ] HIPAA training is completed and documented
- [ ] Policy acknowledgments are signed
- [ ] Mentor is assigned and first 1:1 has occurred
- [ ] First project or task is assigned
- [ ] 30-day check-in is completed

## Exceptions

| Exception | Handling Procedure | Escalation |
|-----------|-------------------|------------|
| Equipment delayed | Provide temporary loaner; escalate to IT leadership | IT Manager |
| New hire cannot complete HIPAA training in Week 1 | Do not assign to client work involving ePHI; extend deadline to Week 2 | HR Coordinator |
| Mentor unavailable (PTO, departure) | Assign alternate mentor immediately | Hiring Manager |
| New hire in a different time zone | Adjust onboarding schedule; use async materials where possible | Hiring Manager |

## Metrics

| Metric | Target | Measurement Method | Frequency |
|--------|--------|--------------------|-----------|
| Time to first contribution | Within 2 weeks | Project management tool | Per new hire |
| HIPAA training completion | 100% by end of Week 1 | Training records | Per new hire |
| 30-day check-in satisfaction | 4/5 or higher | Check-in survey | Per new hire |
| Onboarding checklist completion | 100% within 30 days | Checklist audit | Per new hire |
